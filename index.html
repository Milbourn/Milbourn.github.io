Analytics & BI Portfolio

Hi, I'm Neil. I build BI platforms, the pipelines that feed them, the models underneath, and the dashboards people actually open every morning. I've been doing this for about ten years now, starting at Penguin Random House and more recently leading BI at a UK marketplace agency.

Most days that looks like: pulling data from REST and GraphQL APIs with Python, loading it into Snowflake or Azure SQL, shaping it with dbt, and putting it in front of people through Power BI. I'm based in Paris and work with teams across the UK and EU.

This site has a couple of things I've built and enjoyed working on, more to come as I add them.
